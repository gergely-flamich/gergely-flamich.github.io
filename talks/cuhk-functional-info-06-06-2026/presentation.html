what is a stochastic code?

lossless compression

  • Source: \(X \sim P_X\)
  • Code: \(C(x) \in \{0, 1\}^*\)
  • Decode: \[ C^{-1}(C(x)) = x \]
  • Measure of efficiency: \(\mathbb{E}[\vert C(X) \vert]\)
  • Entropy code: \[\mathbb{E}[\vert C(X) \vert] = \mathbb{H}[P_X] + \mathcal{O}(1)\]

stochastic codes

  • Source: \(X \sim P_X\)
  • Perturbation: \(P_{Y \mid X = x}\)
  • Shared randomness: \(Z \sim P_Z\)
  • Code: \(C(x, z) \in \{0, 1\}^*\)
  • Decode: \[ D(C(x, Z), Z) \sim P_{Y \mid X = x}\]

representing divergences

\(w\) is a PDF! Hence, let \(H \sim w\).

\begin{align*} \mathbb{E}[\log H] &= D_{KL}[Q || P] - \log e \\ \mathbb{E}[H^{s - 1}] &= \frac{1}{s}2^{(s - 1) \cdot D_{s}[Q || P] } \\ \mathbb{P}[H \geq \gamma] &= E_\gamma[Q || P] \end{align*}

What about \(h[H]\)?

channel simulation divergence

\[ D_{CS}[Q || P] \triangleq h[H] \]

Some properties:

  • non-negative
  • \(D_{CS}[Q || P] = 0\) iff \(Q = P\)
  • DPI
  • jointly convex in \((Q, P)\)

more csd properties

\(\mathrm{KL} = D_{KL}[Q || P]\) and \(\mathrm{CS} = D_{CS}[Q || P]\):

\begin{align*} \mathrm{KL} \leq \mathrm{CS} &\leq \mathrm{KL} + \log(\mathrm{KL} + 1) + 1 \\ \mathrm{CS} &\leq D_{\infty}[Q || P] \end{align*}

Furthermore:

\(\mathrm{TV}[Q || P] \leq 1 - 2^{D_{CS}[Q || P]}\)

\(\Lambda(X) = \log |\mathcal{X}| - D_{CS}[P_X || \mathrm{Unif}(\mathcal{X})]\)

functional information

Analogy with MI for \(P_{X, Y}\):

\begin{align*} I[X; Y] &= D_{KL}[P_{X, Y} || P_X P_Y] \\ &= \mathbb{E}_X [D_{KL}[P_{Y \mid X} || P_Y]] \\ &= \mathbb{E}_Y [D_{KL}[P_{X \mid Y} || P_X]] \end{align*}

"Correct" definition:

\[ F[X \to Y] \triangleq \mathbb{E}_Y [D_{CS}[P_{X \mid Y} || P_X]] \]

FI properties

\(F[X \to Y] \triangleq \mathbb{E}_Y [D_{CS}[P_{X \mid Y} || P_X]]\)

Then:

  • \(F[X \to X] = H[X]\) for \(X\) discrete
  • \(F[X \to Y] = 0\) iff \(X \perp Y\)
  • \(F[X \to Y] = I[X; Y]\) iff \(X \to Y\) singular
  • concave in \(P_X\), convex in \(P_{Y \mid X}\)

\[F[X \to (Y, Z)] = F[X \to Y] + F[X \to Z \mid Y] + \Theta(1)\]

main result

\begin{align*} R^* &= \inf_{(Z, C, D)} \mathbb{E}[|C(X, Z)|] \\ &\quad\text{s.t. } D(C(x, Z), Z) \sim P_{Y \mid X = x} \end{align*}

Then, under mild assumptions

\[ F(X \to Y) \leq R^* \leq F(X \to Y) + 2.45 \]

application: rate-distortion

the objective

One-shot rate-distortion function:

\begin{align*} R_O(\delta) &= \inf_{(C, D)} \mathbb{E}[|C(X)|] \\ &\quad \text{s.t. } \mathbb{E}[\Delta(X, D(C(X)))] \leq \delta \end{align*}

applying the FI

Functional rate-distortion function

\begin{align*} R_F(\delta) &= \inf_{P_{Y \mid X}} F[X \to Y] \\ &\quad \text{s.t. } \mathbb{E}[\Delta(X, Y)] \leq \delta \end{align*}

Then:

\(R_F(\delta) \leq R_O(\delta) \leq R_F(\delta) + 3.45\)
